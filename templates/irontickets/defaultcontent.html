Welcome

IronTickets is an open-source web-based ticket management system for IT Solutions Providers. At first glance, you might think IT handles information about your clients and their tickets--but it does so much more.

IronTickets handles your basic client data and tickets, of course--but it also handles several other items:

  • Customer Data
  • Tickets
  • Statistics and Reporting
  • Shop Inventory
  • Client Inventory
  • Managed Service Agreements/Accounts
  • Client self-signup
  • Integration with client authentication (so clients can use their existing windows domain username and password)
  • Projects
  • Ordering and Shipping
  • Tracking all those ticket numbers from other vendors, suppliers, and support companies
  • Document storage
  • Knowledge base
  • Tools to help audit client networks
  • E-Mail integration
  • Outlook calendar integration
  • Outlook contact integration
  • Integration with Nagios monitoring
And there's so much more.
Best of all, it's open source and it's free.
